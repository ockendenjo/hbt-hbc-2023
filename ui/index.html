Instructions

1. Visit a location

Use the Map tab to pick a location to visit. It may be a hidden stash or someone's house/flat. Points as indicated by the numbers on the map markers.

2. Trick or treat

The stash may contain thirst-quenching treats, or might be empty. 1 extra point for consuming the beverage at the location. You aren't allowed to take it away for drinking later.

3. Be back on time

Return to DVC by 18:00. -1 point for every minute late.

Map notes

Bat - normal stash

Pumpkin - house or flat

Orange - may have micro-trot friendly treats (if any left)

Brown - trotter treats (if any left)

Green - you've marked it as visited
