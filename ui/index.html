Instructions

  • Get into a pair/team. Scoring is per team rather than individual.
  • You get points for visiting each stash. The number of points is stated on the icon.
  • Stashes cannot be visited by the same team more than once.
  • Keep track of your teamâ€™s score - this can be done on the app by selecting 'visited' on the drop-down menu. You can also keep track of your bonus points this way! Your score will be shown in the top right corner.
  • If something in the stash has further instructions, you must follow them.
  • There are limited supplies at each stash, so you may arrive to find nothing left. Get in there quickly!
  • You must be back at Bellfield Brewery by 20:30. You lose a point for every minute you are late.

Bonus points

  • 2 points for each beverage drunk at the stop (max 1 beverage per person).
  • Beverages must be drunk on location, but confectionary can be taken away.
