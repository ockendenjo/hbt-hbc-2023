Instructions

1. Visit some pubs

Use the Map tab / Millie's instructions to plan a visit to some pubs or see where HBT takes you.

2. Fill in the form

Fill in the form to submit your pub visits - one form per pub visited.

3. Wait for message from Millie

Millie will notify you if the pub was worth +3 or -1 (but not if +1) within approx 48 hours.

4. Record the points for each pub

From the list tab or grid tab click the pub and record the points scored. Recording points will colour squares in the grid.

5. Use the grid to plan future pub visits

High and negative scoring pubs are arranged in clusters on the grid - this may help you decide which pub(s) to visit next.
